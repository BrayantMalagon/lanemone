About Us

Fleuriste l'anemone is a full-service floral event design studio. We offer our clients a collaborative relationship with a common goal: to create an exceptional event whose memories will remain engraved in the memory.
We work closely with our clients to create specialized authentic design and create a luxurious and impeccable style of arrangements that uniquely reflect each celebration.

Our engagement

We are a company committed to providing quality and impeccable service. Our goal is to bring our clients' ideas to life and support them throughout the process.
