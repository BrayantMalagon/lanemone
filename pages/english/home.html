Services

Fleuriste Ľ'anemone, we offer a personalized and warm service of floral arrangements for all types of events:
Weddings, parties, corporate meetings, funerals, etc.
Our creations come in the form of bouquets, boutonnieres, corsages, hairpieces and more. We also offer accessories such as candles, door wreaths, arches, flower stands, decorative plates and more.
Our chief florist artist Mary Ann, with her 10 years of experience, will make your event an unforgettable day. You will be charmed and won over by his enthusiasm and passion for flowers.
Our greatest strengths lie in our devotion to making your dreams come true, listening to our customers, availability and our speed in responding to text messages, emails and calls. Simply said, you are our priority.
